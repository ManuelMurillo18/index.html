Avantages and Desanvatages of cryptocurrencies
A reason to invest in cryptocurrency is the desire for a reliable, long-term store of value. Unlike fiat money, most cryptocurrencies have a limited supply, capped by mathematical algorithms. This makes it impossible for any political body or government agency to dilute their value through inflation. Risk is so high that you could loose 100% of your money. Some say its like gambling.
